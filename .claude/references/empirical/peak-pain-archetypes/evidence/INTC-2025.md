## A. Industry shock & operational stress  (industry_tailwind = reversed)

10-K Item 1A verbatim: "Cyclical changes in market conditions and the associated pricing environment" combined with "macroeconomic environment and inflationary pressures, with PC supply and demand levels beginning to normalize."

10-K MD&A verbatim: "In Q3 2024, we concluded that our manufacturing asset portfolio, primarily for our Intel 7 process node, exceeded manufacturing capacity requirements" — recorded $3.3B impairment + accelerated depreciation against Intel 7 fab capacity, the canonical signal that internal-fab demand collapsed.

10-K MD&A verbatim: "Q3 2024, we announced our intent to establish Intel Foundry as an independent subsidiary." Foundry-as-subsidiary signaled financial separation from product.

10-K Item 1A verbatim: "Macroeconomic conditions can significantly harm demand for our products and make it more challenging to forecast our operating results and make business decisions, including regarding prioritization of investments."

→ For broad semis the AI wave is INTACT (NVDA, AMD, TSMC at all-time highs). For Intel specifically, the wave is actively *eroding* moat — datacenter share lost to AMD EPYC + NVDA accelerators; AI training spending bypasses Intel; foundry leadership ceded to TSMC. Spec value **reversed** (the structural tailwind that lifted Intel for 40 years has reversed direction); not "structural-decline" because semis demand secularly rising; not "weakening" because this is more than cyclical.

## B. Balance sheet & cash position  (cash_runway = 12-24mo)

10-K verbatim: "Cash and cash equivalents $8,249 [Dec 28, 2024] $7,079 [Dec 30, 2023]. Short-term investments 13,813 17,955. Total cash and short-term investments $22,062 $25,034."

10-K verbatim: "Total debt $50,011 [FY24] $49,266 [FY23]."

10-K verbatim: "We suspended the declaration of quarterly dividends starting with Q4 2024."

10-K verbatim: "In 2024, we invested $16.5 billion in R&D, made gross capital investments of $25.1 billion, and had $8.3 billion in cash from operations and negative $2.2 billion of adjusted free cash flow."

10-K verbatim: "Net proceeds from partner contributions 11,012 1,702" (Apollo SCIP / Brookfield SCIP / partner financing).

→ Liquid assets $22B; debt $50B (5x liquid); FCF -$2.2B; net of expected partner contributions and CHIPS Act capital, runway extends roughly 12-24mo before need to materially restructure or raise. Dividend suspension is the textbook signal of cash-flow stress. The catalog row "fragile" maps to **12-24mo** in-domain (not "<12mo" because Intel still has $22B liquid at FY24 close; not "distressed" because Intel is not in default and has investment-grade debt rating, though stressed).

## E. Margin & revenue trajectory  (margin_trajectory = deteriorating; revenue_trajectory = declining)

10-K verbatim: "Net revenue $53,101 [FY24] $54,228 [FY23] $63,054 [FY22]. Cost of sales 35,756 32,517 36,188. Gross margin 17,345 21,711 26,866."

→ GM% calc: 17,345/53,101 = **32.7%** FY24; 21,711/54,228 = **40.0%** FY23; 26,866/63,054 = **42.6%** FY22. -700bps GM collapse FY23→FY24, -1000bps from peak. **Deteriorating** unambiguous.

10-K verbatim: "Net income (loss) $(19,233) [FY24] $1,675 [FY23] $8,017 [FY22]. Net income (loss) attributable to Intel $(18,756) [FY24] $1,689 [FY23] $8,014 [FY22]."

→ FY24 net loss $18.8B (vs $1.7B income prior year, $8.0B FY22). Revenue $53.1B FY24 vs $63.1B FY22 peak = **-16% peak-to-trough**; FY24 vs FY23 = -2% (small but two consecutive years of decline). **Declining** unambiguous.

## F. Operational restructuring (idiosyncratic context)

10-K verbatim: "2024 Restructuring Plan that are expected to result in an approximate 15% decrease in our core Intel workforce by early 2025."

10-K verbatim: "As a result of initiating and deploying our 2024 Restructuring Plan, we recognized restructuring charges of $2.8 billion in 2024."

10-K verbatim: "$3.3 billion of charges, substantially all of which were recorded to cost of sales, related to non-cash impairments and the acceleration of depreciation for certain manufacturing assets, a substantial majority of which related to our Intel 7 process node."

10-K verbatim: "$3.1 billion of non-cash charges associated with the impairment of" goodwill (subsequent text: against the Mobileye and Altera/PSG segments).

→ Combined $9B+ of charges in FY24. 15% workforce reduction in core Intel + foundry separation + dividend suspension + non-cash impairments = textbook deep-restructuring trough year.
