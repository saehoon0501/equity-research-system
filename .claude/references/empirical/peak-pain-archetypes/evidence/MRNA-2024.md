## A. Industry shock & operational stress (industry_tailwind = weakening)

10-K verbatim: "Total revenue decreased by $3.6 billion, or 53%, in 2024, primarily attributable to a significant reduction in net product sales of our COVID vaccine. Net product sales decreased by $3.6 billion, or 53%, in 2024, mainly due to lower sales volumes in Europe and the rest of the world."

10-K verbatim: "Currently, our COVID and RSV vaccines are our only commercial products and our RSV sales have been minimal." Demand cliff is post-pandemic seasonalization, not platform failure — mRESVIA RSV approved May 2024 evidences pipeline still advancing. Tailwind has weakened from FY21–22 pandemic-vaccine boom; NOT "reversed" (mRNA platform thesis intact, two approved products) and NOT "structural-decline" (oncology/RSV pipeline progressing).

## B. Balance sheet & cash position (cash_runway = >24mo)

10-K verbatim: "As of December 31, 2024, we had approximately $9.5 billion in cash, cash equivalents and investments."

FY2024 net loss $(9.28) per share × ~386M shares ≈ $3.6B annual loss. $9.5B / $3.6B ≈ 2.6 years of unaffected runway at FY24 burn — and management announced cost-reduction actions targeting GAAP operating-cost cuts of ~$1B by 2027, extending runway. No material funded debt. Cash position therefore comfortably **>24mo**.

## C. Strategic context (NOT a universal-core feature; archetype-matching only)

mRESVIA (mRNA-1345 RSV vaccine) FDA approval May 2024 — second commercial product. Pipeline diversification: mRNA-1083 (combo flu+COVID), mRNA-4157 (individualized neoantigen oncology w/ Merck), CMV vaccine, rare-disease programs. Sector-extension `pipeline_optionality` territory; for universal-core extraction use only the FY24 demand-reset framing, not the pipeline thesis.

## D. Founder/insider behavior (founder_in_place = yes; founder_insider_stake_direction = flat)

10-K verbatim references "Stephane Bancel" as Chief Executive Officer in 2024 trading-arrangement disclosures (10b5-1 plan termination filed Nov 8, 2024). Bancel co-founded Moderna's modern operating form as CEO since 2011 and remains CEO at FY24 close.

→ **founder_in_place = "yes"** (single canonical spec value).

Stake direction: Bancel continues 10b5-1 program sales (e.g., Feb 14, 2024 sale of 26,351 shares for $2.34M; cumulative ~$408M sold since 2020 under pre-pandemic plans). Individual transaction-level sales occurred but in an automated 10b5-1 framework adopted Dec 2018 — not a thesis-shifting stake-decrease event. No documented FY24 stake-build event either. Treating as **flat** under conservative spec mapping (program-driven dilution/sales without a discretionary build).

→ **founder_insider_stake_direction = "flat"** (single canonical spec value).

## E. Margin & revenue trajectory (margin_trajectory = improving; revenue_trajectory = declining)

10-K verbatim: "Cost of sales was $1.5 billion, or 47% of our net product sales, in 2024, including third-party royalties of $155 million, inventory write-downs of $495 million… wind-down costs of $263 million, unutilized manufacturing capacity of $105 million."

10-K verbatim: "Cost of sales was $4.7 billion, or 70% of our net product sales, in 2023, including… inventory write-downs of $2.2 billion."

→ COGS ratio 70%→47% = +23pp gross-margin improvement y/y (driven by smaller write-downs and rationalized capacity). **Improving** at FY24 close — single unambiguous spec value.

10-K revenue: 2024 net product sales $3.1B vs 2023 $6.7B vs 2022 $18.4B. Total revenue 2024 $3.236B = -53% y/y.

→ **revenue_trajectory = "declining"** (single canonical spec value; -53% y/y unambiguously crosses the "declining" threshold).

## F. Operational specifics — write-downs and capacity wind-down

10-K verbatim: "inventory write-downs of $495 million, primarily for raw materials and our finished and semi-finished COVID vaccine inventory, wind-down costs of $263 million, unutilized manufacturing capacity of $105 million." This is the residual COVID-capacity normalization tail — the FY23 write-downs were larger ($2.2B); FY24 reflects partial digestion. Maps to `margin_trajectory = improving` magnitude (off a deeply-impaired base).
